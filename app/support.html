Frequently asked questions

  • How do I set up Smart Teddy?

    • Turn on your Teddy.
    • Download My Smart Teddy app for iOS from the App Store or for Android from Google Play, turn on your Bluetooth and grant permission to use geolocation.
    • Complete your registration in the app: enter your telephone number and the verification code that was texted to you or log in using your Google or Facebook account.
    • Find your Teddy in the application. In order to synchronize it, you will have to press both bottom paws at the same time when the application asks you to.
    • If you cannot connect to your Teddy, he may already be synchronized with another smartphone.
    • Deactivate the existing connection and try to connect again.
